About

Captions, but better.

Capto is an AI image captioning app that helps turn your photos into social-media-ready captions in seconds. Upload an image, choose a tone like default, funny, poetic, witty, or formal, and Capto gives you a caption you can edit, copy, or download.

Instead of using generic templates, Capto reads the image itself and builds captions around what is actually in the frame. The whole experience is simple and focused: upload, preview, generate, tweak, and use your caption without fighting through a messy workflow.
